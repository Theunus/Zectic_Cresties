About Us

Welcome to Zectic Cresties

A family-based crested gecko breeding facility dedicated to producing healthy, high-quality geckos with stunning genetics and vibrant colors.

What started as a passion for these fascinating reptiles quickly grew into a mission to provide exceptional care, ethical breeding practices, and education for fellow gecko enthusiasts.

At Zectic Cresties, we believe that every gecko deserves a loving home

We take pride in raising well-socialized, healthy cresties with strong lineage and unique traits. Our family is committed to maintaining a clean, enriching environment for our geckos, ensuring they thrive from hatchling to adulthood.

Whether you're a seasoned reptile keeper or new to the world of crested geckos, we're here to help you find the perfect addition to your collection. Explore our available geckos, learn more about care and husbandry, and join us in celebrating these incredible creatures!
